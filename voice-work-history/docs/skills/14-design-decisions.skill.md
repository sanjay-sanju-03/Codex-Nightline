# Design Decisions

## Why voice?

Speaking can be faster and more natural than completing a typed form after a shift.

## Why review?

The worker owns the work history; AI suggestions require correction and confirmation.

## Why local storage?

It reduces onboarding friction and keeps MVP records on the current device.

## Why no login?

The MVP tests voice-to-record usefulness, not account management or cloud synchronization.

## Why a narrow MVP?

Validate repeat use and worker value before adding verification, finance, or external workflows.

## Core Principle

**AI extracts. The worker confirms.**
